Denzel Curry


Denzel Rae Don Curry (born February 16, 1995) is an American rapper from Carol City, Florida. He released his debut full-length album, titled Nostalgic 64 on September 3, 2013. His second full-length album, titled Imperial, was released on March 9, 2016.
